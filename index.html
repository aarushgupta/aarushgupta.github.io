Biography

I am a third-year undergraduate student at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, majoring in Computer Science & Engineering. My interests include Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and the application of Deep Learning in these fields.

At IIT Roorkee, I worked with Dr. Biplab Banerjee on Human Action Detection in Videos and Zero-Shot Learning. I also worked with Dr. Marco Pedersoli and Jose Dolz at ETS Montreal in the summer of 2018 on Group-Level Emotion Recognition. I recently interned at the Video Analytics Lab in IISc Bangalore where I worked on developing a neural network to generate HDR images from exposure stacks.

I’ll be joining the Visual Computing Group at Harvard University in May 2019 for my summer internship. I have also been selected for the SN Bose Scholars Program 2019.
